THE STORY

They never expected to meet their spouses at a speed dating event...

08
MAY
2015

SPEED DATING

He was new in town and looking for a way to meet new people. She was accompanying a friend and not planning on participating in the event. He arrived early and she was at the bar watching a Nationals baseball game. They struck up a conversation and had plans before the event started. By the end of the evening, they already had plans for a date... though only he chose her on the speed dating form.

15
MAY
2015

THE FIRST DATE

Despite a torrential rain, they both eventually made it to their destination. Drinks turned into dinner, and dinner turned into a nightcap and popcorn at a dive bar in Chinatown. Six hours later, they called it a night with a brief kiss. Neither could have realized at the time that 2 months and multiple Nationals games later, they would say, "I love you."

25
DEC
2015

OUR FIRST HOLIDAYS

The first holidays were spent on a whirlwind tour of the East Coast visiting family over the course of two weeks. After time with his mother in Alexandria, it was off to Myrtle Beach for Christmas. Next was Atlanta, where she met his father. Then a plane flight to Miami for the Orange Bowl on New Year's Eve, where they celebrated Clemson's win in the College Football Playoffs.
08
APRIL
2016

HE PROPOSED

He started a new job in Charleston in March, but she still had 3 months of school left in Northern Virginia so much of the spring involved JetBlue flights on the weekends. She knew a proposal was coming but wasn't expecting to see him until the next weekend. With the help of the staff at her school, he gave her quite a surprise and was able to get the moment on video.

02
JULY
2016

SHE MOVED TO CHARLESTON

At the end of June, she was able to join him on their next adventure. They packed up a van and headed south. Arriving in SC on the Fourth of July weekend, things got off to a wild start. His office burned down on Independence Day. She experienced her first hurricane evacuation 2 months later. Life hasn't slowed down yet for the happy couple, but they are loving every minute together.

CEREMONY

Friday @ 5:30 p.m.


We are flipping the script on a normal wedding weekend. The ceremony will start things on Friday evening at the Confederate Home and College in downtown Charleston. The Confederate Home is located at 62 Broad St, just a few feet away from Washington Square Park.

RECEPTION

Friday @ 7:00 p.m.


The reception will also take place in Downtown Charleston on Friday evening. The location is Upstairs at Midtown, 559 King St. Due to the size of the location, the reception will be for ages 18+ only. Please contact us if you need help finding a babysitter.

BEACH PARTY

Saturday @ ~3:30 p.m.


The other half of our flipped script will include a Saturday afternoon beach party that everyone is invited to attend. We have rented a oceanfront house at 601 W Ashley Ave in Folly Beach. There will be oysters, drinks, games and Lowcountry Boil for dinner.

KING CHARLES INN

The King Charles Inn is located downtown within walking distance of the reception. Rates for a queen room are $239 on Thursday and $419 on Friday/Saturday. A room with either a king bed or 2 double beds in $20 more per night.

Tides Folly Beach is a beachfront hotel about 3 blocks from the beach house that will be the site of the Beach Party on Saturday. The hotel doesn't provide block resservations or group rates, but I was currently able to find rooms for $310/night using a AAA discount.

FRED HOLLAND REALTY

Fred Holland Realty helped us find the house that we are renting for the beach party on Saturday so we wanted to send some additional business there way. Be aware that they may not have set there rates for 2017 yet.

VRBO OR AIRBNB

VRBO and Airbnb are two additional options for finding rental houses on Folly Beach. The VRBO link above has ~60 houses available on the right dates that are near the beach party house.
